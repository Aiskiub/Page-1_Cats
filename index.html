BLACK

Black cats are shy, calm, very intuitive and affectionate. Over the years, in many cultures have believed that black cats are a sign of bad luck.

YELLOW

Orange cats tend to be very sociable and outgoing as well as active and playful. They are ideal for families with children

BROWN

Brown cats can be quite independent, they enjoy exploring their surroundings and can be very curious about anything that catches their eye.
